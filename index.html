Summy

Introducing Summy, the text summarization tool designed to address the limitations of existing platforms. Unlike other sites that impose character or token caps, Summy breaks free from these restrictions, allowing users to effortlessly summarize extensive volumes of text without any limitations. With Summy, you can now conquer lengthy paragraphs, papers, articles, and even entire books, all in record time, experiencing up to 90% faster reading speeds.

How does Summy achieve this extraordinary feat? It efficiently encodes text into manageable chunks of tokens. These tokenized segments are then processed through an AWS Lambda serverless function, purpose-built to deliver accurate and concise summarizations.

With Summy, you can bid farewell to the daunting task of sifting through overwhelming amounts of information and instead embrace a streamlined, efficient approach to absorbing content.

Words With Friends Solver

This Python application is designed specifically for Words with Friends and Scrabble enthusiasts: an indispensable tool that provides a competitive edge by enabling users to effortlessly identify the highest-scoring playable words on a game board. Inspired by the research of Andrew W. Appel and Guy Jacobson, renowned for their development of "The World's Fastest Scrabble Algorithm," this application implements their algorithm to deliver both speed and accuracy.

At the core of our application lies a trie created to facilitate the generation of prefixes and suffixes for each tile. This approach not only ensures efficient and fast word exploration but also takes into account various edge cases that often arise in gameplay such as handling board modifiers, special tiles, and preexisting letter flagging.

This application empowers users to gain a strategic advantage in their Words with Friends and Scrabble matches. Say goodbye to time-consuming manual word searches and hello to a streamlined and dynamic experience, where the highest-scoring playable words are just a click away. Embrace this invaluable tool and elevate your gameplay to new heights.

Wander: Travel Spontaneously

Wander is an online travel agency web application that revolutionizes the way users plan and book their trips. By harnessing the power of generative AI, specifically the GPT4 API, our application intelligently analyzes user preferences to curate and book personalized travel experiences.

Only 43% of US citizens hold a US passport, which is a rapidly decreasing percentage since the 70s. This is in stark contrast to the passport holding percentage of 60-70% of Canadians, and 70-80% of Europeans, Australians, and New Zealanders. It's also estimated that one in nine Americans will never leave their own state. This lack of travel has become a problem for the US as a nation. Our vision is to encourage spontaneity and foster cultural understanding, allowing users to embark on unforgettable journeys tailored to their unique tastes.

Wander seamlessly integrates with the Amadeus Travel GDS and leverages various REST APIs to access a vast database of approximately 400,000 geography definitions. By dynamically filtering and selecting from over 35 different property categories and a staggering 75 million traveler ratings, Wander provides unique and tailored recommendations for each user.
