Summy

Most text summarization sites have character or token caps making it difficult to summarize large amounts of text. Summy fixes this, giving users quick and reliable text summarizations with no limits. Summy can help you read paragraphs, papers, articles, or even books within in as up to 90% faster.

Encodes text into chunks of tokens, calls custom AWS Lambda serverless function on each chunk, and returns summarization.
